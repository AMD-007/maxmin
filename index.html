Teliti Infotech: Complete Digital Transformation

How we redesigned Malaysia's leading IT solutions provider's website to drive business growth

Duration: 3 months Industry: IT Solutions Services: Web Design, Development, SEO

Executive Summary

Teliti Infotech, a leading IT solutions provider in Malaysia, needed a complete website transformation to better reflect their market leadership and generate qualified leads. We delivered a modern, responsive website that increased conversions by 68% while improving user experience across all devices.

68% Increase in Lead Generation
42% Faster Page Load
3.2x Mobile Engagement

The Challenge

Challenges Faced

  • Outdated design not reflecting their market leadership position
  • Poor mobile experience with 45% bounce rate on mobile devices
  • Low conversion rates (1.2% form submissions)
  • Ineffective content structure for complex service offerings
  • Slow page load times affecting user experience and SEO

Our Solution

  • Mobile-first responsive design approach
  • Streamlined user journey with clear CTAs
  • Modern design system reflecting corporate identity
  • Performance optimization for faster loading
  • SEO-enhanced structure and content

Our Process

We applied our proven 5-step methodology to ensure project success:

  1. Discovery & Strategy: Comprehensive analysis of business goals, target audience, and competitor landscape
  2. Design & Prototyping: Created wireframes and interactive prototypes for stakeholder feedback
  3. Development: Built with modern technologies ensuring scalability and performance
  4. Testing & Optimization: Rigorous testing across devices and browsers
  5. Launch & Support: Smooth deployment with ongoing maintenance and support
